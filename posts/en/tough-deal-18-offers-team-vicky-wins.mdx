Another happy client, another successful closing — and this one was anything but easy. Team Vicky is proud to share a recent win that shows what strategic representation can do in a highly competitive market.

## 18 Offers. 1 Winner.

This home attracted **18 competing offers** — a level of competition that can feel overwhelming for any buyer. In a market like this, it's not just about offering the highest price. It's about crafting an offer that stands out on every level: terms, timing, contingencies, and communication with the listing agent.

> In a multiple-offer situation, the strongest offer isn't always the highest one. It's the one the seller trusts most.

## $10,000 Seller Credit — On Top of Winning

Winning the bid was only half the story. Through careful negotiation during the inspection and escrow process, Team Vicky secured an additional **$10,000 credit from the seller** — money that went directly toward the buyer's closing costs.

That's the difference experienced representation makes: not just getting you the home, but protecting your financial interests throughout the entire transaction.
