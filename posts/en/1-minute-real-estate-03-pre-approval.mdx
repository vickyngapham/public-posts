## Why You Should Get Pre-Approval Before House Hunting

Most people start their home-buying journey by browsing listings and scheduling tours. But in the U.S., the smartest first step is actually getting **mortgage pre-approval**. Here are four reasons why.

### ✔️ Shop Within Your Real Budget

Pre-approval tells you exactly how much you can borrow. This keeps you focused on homes you can actually afford — saving you time and avoiding disappointment.

### ✔️ Submit Stronger Offers

In competitive markets like San Diego, sellers receive multiple offers. A buyer with a pre-approval letter signals financial readiness and seriousness, giving your offer a significant advantage.

> Sellers are far more likely to accept an offer backed by a lender's confirmation.

### ✔️ Understand Your Loan Terms Upfront

Pre-approval reveals your estimated interest rate, monthly payment, and loan limits before you start shopping. No surprises down the road.

### ✔️ Reduce the Risk of a Failed Transaction

One of the most common reasons deals fall through is financing denial at the last minute. Pre-approval dramatically reduces this risk by confirming your eligibility early in the process.
